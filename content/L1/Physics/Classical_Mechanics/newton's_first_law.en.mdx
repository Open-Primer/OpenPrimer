# Newton's First Law : Deep Academic Dive

<SelfEval title="Newton's First Law" type="pre" />

## 1. Core Principles
Newton's First Law is the study of how **systems** maintain **stability** under stress. In **Physics**, this is measured by the **Alpha-Gradient**. 
Imagine a bridge: its stability isn't just about the strength of steel, but the **distribution of forces**. 

<MetaNote title="Analogy">
  A system in equilibrium is like a dancer on a tightrope: constantly moving to remain still.
</MetaNote>
