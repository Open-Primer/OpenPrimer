# Introduction to Classical Mechanics : Foundation Overview

Welcome to the **Classical Mechanics** foundation course. This module introduces the basic kinematic and dynamic equations governing physical bodies at rest and in motion.

<SelfEval title="Classical Mechanics Introduction" type="pre" />

## 1. Scope of Classical Mechanics
Classical Mechanics is the bedrock of physics, modeling the trajectory of bodies under the influence of force fields. It scales from structural engineering to planetary motions.

<MetaNote title="Analogy">
  Forces are the verbs of the physical universe; mass is the noun, and acceleration is the adverb.
</MetaNote>
