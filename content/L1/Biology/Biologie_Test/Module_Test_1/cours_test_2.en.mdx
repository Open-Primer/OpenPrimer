## 1. ATP Synthesis and Cellular Respiration
The mitochondria are cellular power plants where glucose and oxygen are metabolized to produce ATP via the **Krebs cycle** and **oxidative phosphorylation**.

<MetaNote title="Analogy">
  ATP acts as the chemical currency of the cell, spent whenever a mechanical or chemical task is executed.
</MetaNote>
