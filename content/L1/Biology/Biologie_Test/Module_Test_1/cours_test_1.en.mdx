## 1. Cell Membrane Fluidity
The cell membrane consists of a double layer of lipids with proteins floating in it, known as the **Fluid Mosaic Model**. It regulates what enters and leaves the cell.

<MetaNote title="Key Concept">
  Hydrophobic tails face inward, while hydrophilic heads face the aqueous external environment.
</MetaNote>
