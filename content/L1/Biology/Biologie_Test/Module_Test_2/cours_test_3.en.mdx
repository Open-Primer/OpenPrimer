## 1. Transcription and mRNA Synthesis
The central dogma of molecular biology describes the flow of genetic information: DNA is transcribed into RNA, which is then translated into proteins.

<MetaNote title="Analogy">
  If DNA is the master architectural blueprint stored securely in the library vault, mRNA is a photocopied page taken out to the construction site.
</MetaNote>
