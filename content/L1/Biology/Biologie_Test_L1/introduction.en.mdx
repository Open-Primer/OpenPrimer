## 1. DNA Structure and Double Helix
DNA is composed of two polynucleotide chains coiled around a common axis, forming a **double helix**.

<MetaNote title="Key Insight">
  Adenine always pairs with Thymine, and Cytosine always pairs with Guanine.
</MetaNote>
