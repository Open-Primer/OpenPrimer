## 1. Concept of Sovereignty
Sovereignty refers to the supreme power or authority of a state to govern itself.

<MetaNote title="Analogy">
  Constitutional law is like the operating system of a nation, while civil laws are the individual applications running on top of it.
</MetaNote>
