## 1. Core Calculus
Calculus models change. The derivative of a function $f(x)$ represents its instantaneous rate of change:
$$f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}$$

<MetaNote title="Key Insight">
  Integrals calculate the area under curves and are linked to derivatives by the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
</MetaNote>
