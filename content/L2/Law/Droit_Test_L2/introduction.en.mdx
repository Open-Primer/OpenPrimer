## 1. Principles of Criminal Liability
A crime consists of two elements: the physical act (*actus reus*) and the mental intent (*mens rea*).

<MetaNote title="Key Insight">
  Both elements must concur to establish criminal liability under modern penal systems.
</MetaNote>
