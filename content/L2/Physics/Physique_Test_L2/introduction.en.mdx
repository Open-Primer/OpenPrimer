## 1. Wave-Particle Duality
All matter exhibits both wave and particle properties. This is modeled by the Schrödinger equation:
$$i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\Psi = \hat{H}\Psi$$

<MetaNote title="Key Insight">
  Unlike classical systems, quantum mechanics is fundamentally probabilistic.
</MetaNote>
