# 🗺️ OpenPrimer Industrial Syllabus Overview

This document outlines the academic structure and content density targets for the OpenPrimer platform.

## 🎓 Academic Hierarchy
OpenPrimer follows a standardized University structure (Bachelor/Licence L1-L3).

### [L1] Foundation Level
Focuses on core terminology, basic principles, and historical context.
- **Target Density**: 20 hours per module.
- **Validation**: 4/8 modules required for UV validation.

### [L2] Intermediate Level
Deepens technical understanding with mathematical demonstrations and case studies.
- **Target Density**: 30 hours per module.
- **Validation**: 6/10 modules required.

### [L3] Advanced Level
Specialized topics and research-oriented content.
- **Target Density**: 50 hours per module.
- **Validation**: Final project + elective modules.
